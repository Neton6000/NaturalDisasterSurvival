    Tricks and puzzles

    Various tips, tricks and reminders so you can get better!

    • You should never go to a high place before you know what it will be, otherwise it will be difficult for you if it starts e.g. burn or become an earthquake.
    • Do not sit in Cars, especially those in Raving Raceway, because the cars can start to glide so you are thrown into space.
    • If there is a tsunami, you still do not have to climb so high. Sit almost exactly so that you do not touch the water.
    • At Coastal Quickstop you can go to the lighthouse or a dock if a tornado comes, it's because things are off the island so then tornadoes can not reach them.
    • You should Never go into the space rocket on Launch Land, because then you risk dying if someone clicks on the launch button.
